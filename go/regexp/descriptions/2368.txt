'クレイグ・マーフィー' ('Craig N. Murphy')は、ウェズリー女子大学政治学部教授。専攻は、国際政治経済学、南北問題。1980年、ノースカロライナ大学より博士号取得。

==著作==
===単著===
*''The Emergence of the NIEO Ideology'', (Westview Press, 1984).
*''International Organization and Industrial Change: Global Governance since 1850'', (Polity Press, 1994).
*''The United Nations Development Programme: A Better Way?'', (Cambridge University Press, 2006).
**峯陽一・小山田英治監訳『国連開発計画（UNDP）の歴史』（明石書店、2014）

===共著===
*''America's Quest for Supremacy and the Third World: A Gramscian Analysis'', with Enrico Augelli, (Pinter, 1988).
*''The International Organization for Standardization (ISO): global governance through voluntary consensus'', with JoAnne Yates, Routledge, 2009.

===編著===
*''Egalitarian Politics in the Age of Globalization'', (Palgrave, 2002).
*''Global Institutions, Marginalization and Development'', (Routledge, 2004).

===共編著===
*''The New International Political Economy'', co-edited with Roger Tooze, (Lynne Rienner, 1991).
*''International Relations and the New Inequality'', co-edited with Mustapha Kamal Pasha, (Blackwell, 2002).
